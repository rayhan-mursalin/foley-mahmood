About

Foley and Mahmood P.C. has a national practice in the field of immigration law. We represent clients in state and federal criminal law, civil cases and domestic relation and general practice of law. The firm has a concentration in the practice of US immigration law in all aspects, deportation, defenses, and represents clients regularly before the state, federal, and various administrative agencies, including the immigration courts, board of immigration appeal, and US department of Labor and US department of states in obtaining visas for our clients. Complete client satisfaction and representing clients best interest before the state, federal and other government agencies within the bounds of law is the guiding principle in our law firm.

Here at Foley and Mahmood we look forward to assist you using law and regulation in the United states and in the overseas in your actions in the field of international law and other legal actions.At Foley and Mahmood law firm our dedication is to provide best possible legal representation to our clients and as active member of the State bar of Alabama, we observe all the rules and canons. We also state that we make no representation that our services will be superior to others in the practice of law per State bar regulations.

You can contact us for all your legal needs including the legal matters in the overseas, where our firm has strong affiliation with reputed law firms and chambers in the overseas including the United Kingdom, Canada, and Saudi Arabia.
